About Our Library

Our History

Founded in 1925, Evergreen Community Library has been a cornerstone of education and community engagement for nearly a century. From our humble beginnings as a single reading room to our modern facility today, we've always been committed to fostering knowledge and lifelong learning.

Our Collection

With over 50,000 books, periodicals, audiobooks, and digital resources, our diverse collection caters to readers of all ages and interests. We're constantly updating our materials to reflect the needs and interests of our community.

Our Mission

We believe in providing free and equal access to information, promoting literacy, and creating an inclusive environment where everyone feels welcome. Our mission is to empower individuals through knowledge and foster community connections.

Our Services

Book Borrowing

Borrow up to 10 items for 3 weeks with your library card. Renew online or in person up to 2 times if there are no holds.

Computer Access

Use our computers and free Wi-Fi for research, job searching, or personal projects. Printing services available for a small fee.

Study Rooms

Reserve a quiet study room for individual or group work. Available in 2-hour blocks and can be booked up to 2 weeks in advance.

Reference Services

Get expert help with research from our knowledgeable librarians. Available in person, by phone, or via email.
